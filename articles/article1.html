On Minimal Interfaces

Essay · 2026

Introduction

Minimal interfaces focus on clarity and removing friction. They are not about removing personality, but about prioritizing content and action.

Principles

  • Prioritize content hierarchy.
  • Reduce choice where possible.
  • Design for readable, scannable text.

Examples

Use generous whitespace, clear typographic scale, and intentional color accents to guide attention.
